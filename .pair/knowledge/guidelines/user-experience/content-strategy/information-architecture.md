# Information Architecture

## Introduction

Information architecture (IA) is the structural design of shared information environments, focusing on organizing, structuring, and labeling content in an effective and sustainable way. In user experience design, IA helps users find information and complete tasks by creating logical, navigable structures that align with user mental models and business objectives.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Content organization and categorization systems
- Navigation structure and hierarchy design
- Labeling and taxonomy development
- Information flow and user journey mapping
- Search and findability optimization
- Mental model research and application
- Cross-platform information consistency
- Content relationship mapping
- User task flow analysis
- Information accessibility and inclusion

### Out of Scope
